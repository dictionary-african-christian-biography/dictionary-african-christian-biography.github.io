Mission: Recovery and Resourcing

The DACB Project aims to recover, preserve, and disseminate the biographies of the mothers and fathers of African Christian history. By documenting this missing chapter of global Christian history, our work highlights the crucial role of Africa in World Christian history. Read our vision and mission here.

The DACB Vision

To Shift Perspectives

Since 2018, Africa is the continent with the most Christians. The DACB highlights the fundamental centrality of the African Christian witness in the growth of the African and global church. Read stories here.

To Connect People

We collaborate with African writers and institutions in the mission of documenting African Christian biographies, by networking and offering resources and training. Check out our resources and how to support our work.

To Foster More Just Relationships

Our free history resources and publication opportunities for rising African scholars and church practitioners help to create a more equitable educational experience. Read and download our Journal here.
