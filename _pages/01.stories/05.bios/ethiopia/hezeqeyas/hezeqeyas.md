Hezeqeyas, *Abba*, became a saint of the Ethiopian Church. A disciple of *Abba* 'Iyäsus-Mo'a, he is mentioned as having preached, with *Abba* Gäbrä-'Endreyas, from Wägda to Gozam (Gojjam).

G. W. B. Huntingford
