Euprepius, *Abunä*, was a bishop in Ethiopia during the reign of Emperor Kaléb (r. c. 514-30). A certain Umayya, a refugee form Najran, begged assistance from him and Kaléb against Dh&uuml; Nuw&auml;s.

A. K. Irvine
