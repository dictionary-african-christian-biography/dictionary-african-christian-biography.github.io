Giyorgis, *Abba*, of Däbrä Bahrey, became a saint of the Ethiopian Church. He was a disciple of *Abba* 'Iyäsus Mo'a, and preached from Wäläqa to Sägla (perhaps Sägla in Endärta).

G. W. B. Huntingford
