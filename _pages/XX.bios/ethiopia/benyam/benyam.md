Benyam, *Abba* (perhaps fl. c. 10th century A.D.), was a bishop who, according to the *Gädlä Iyäsus-Mo'a*, came at some time after Sälama Zä-'Azéb and also gave his benediction to the monastery of Däbrä Hayq.

Belaynesh Michael
